About Me

I’m a front-end web developer with back-end experience, passionate about UI/UX design with strong skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Bootstrap, and Semantic UI. I’ve worked as an independent developer and designer for over three years and have built custom Ruby on Rails and WordPress themes for client projects and web sites without any framework or CMS. I especially enjoy discovery process, user research, wire-framing, and design on web projects.

I followed my passions a few years ago and transitioned from the fine art world to web development. I am currently an interactive creative director where I enjoy both coding and the visual design aspects of building web sites. My experience in small startup technology companies has taught me how to collaborate effectively, handle multiple jobs and titles while working both in-person and as a remote team member to get the job done at a moment’s notice.
